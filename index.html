First Aid Tips & Advice

If first aiders is not available, follow these steps:

  • Stay calm and call emergency services if necessary.
  • Check for responsiveness and breathing.
  • Apply pressure to any bleeding wounds.
  • Keep the injured person comfortable and warm.
